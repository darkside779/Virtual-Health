Dr. Sara Abdalrhman

Gynecologist

Dr. Sara Abdalrhman is a highly experienced gynecologist with extensive expertise in her field. She graduated from Cairo University, where she received comprehensive training in diagnosing and managing a wide range of gynecological and obstetric conditions. Dr. Abdalrhman is known for her holistic approach to patient care, combining precise diagnostics with personalized treatment plans. She has a strong background in managing complex pregnancies and chronic gynecological conditions.


Experience and Expertise:
- Over 17 years of experience in gynecology and obstetrics
- Expertise in managing complex gynecological cases and providing comprehensive care.
- Proficient in minimally invasive surgical techniques and advanced diagnostic methods.
- Dedicated to preventive care and personalized health education.
